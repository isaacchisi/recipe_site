Time Prepartion Cooking Duration
10min 20min 30min 60min

Ingredients

Dark Chocolate: 200g

Flour: 200g

Butter: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Eggs: 4

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

Mix the sugar,eggs and the flour

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven. Melt the chocolate and the butter together
